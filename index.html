Projects

VICCI

VICCI is a programmable cloud-computing research testbed, encompassing both a distributed set of virtualized compute clusters and networking hardware, and the software that enables multiple researchers to innovate both at and above the infrastructure layer. VICCI is designed to support research both into the design, provisioning, and management of a global, multi-datacenter infrastructure---the cloud---as well as the design and deployment of large-scale distributed services that use such an environment.

PlanetLab

PlanetLab is a global research network that supports the development of new network services. Since the beginning of 2003, more than 1,000 researchers at top academic institutions and industrial research labs have used PlanetLab to develop new technologies for distributed storage, network mapping, peer-to-peer systems, distributed hash tables, and query processing.

GENICloud

The work of my master’s thesis (available here). GENICloud is an on-going project involving HP Labs, Planet Works (Princeton), and UVic. The project enables the federation of GENI and Eucalyptus. Specifically, PlanetLab users can dynamically provision their slices with resources from a federated Eucalyptus cloud. Also, we are developing a service to facilitate the federation of testbeds and cloud systems.
